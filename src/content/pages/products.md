# cue-bot

*A personal assistance agent.*

cue-bot is one agent that works across the parts of your life that don't fit neatly into any single app — planning meals, keeping tabs on what's happening at home, drafting messages, remembering the things you said you'd do.

The same conversation stays coherent as new domains get added, which is most of the point.
